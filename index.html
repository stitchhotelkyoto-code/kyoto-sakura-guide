Spring Highlights in Kyoto

京都の桜スポットを、雰囲気・混雑傾向・写真検索とあわせてご覧いただけます。

Top Sakura Spot

Philosopher's Path

Peaceful canal-side path lined with cherry blossoms.

Best time: Early morning. Usually busiest from late morning to mid-afternoon.

Popular

Maruyama Park

Kyoto’s iconic hanami location with a classic spring atmosphere.

Best time: Before 9:00 AM. Crowds usually build from midday into the evening.

Keage Incline

Best time: Morning. The area tends to feel busiest around late morning and early afternoon.

Arashiyama

A famous sakura area with river, bridge, and mountain scenery.

Best time: Very early morning. This area is often most crowded from around 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

A relaxed riverside walk with seasonal cherry blossom scenery.

Best time: Morning or near sunset. It is usually more relaxed than major tourist hotspots.

A classic Kyoto temple view with spring cherry blossoms.

Best time: Early morning. This area can become very busy from late morning through the afternoon.
